# 2 Mock Data and SubWorkflow

> Retrieves customer data from Google Sheets based on an email address and determines their VIP status.

---

## 📌 Overview

This workflow is designed to be executed as a sub-workflow by a parent or main workflow. It accepts a customer's email address as an input parameter and queries a Google Sheets spreadsheet to retrieve the corresponding customer record.

Once the customer data is retrieved, the workflow evaluates the customer's rating and total order count to determine and assign a VIP status. This status is then appended to the customer's profile data for downstream processing.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The business purpose of this workflow is to centralize and automate the retrieval of customer profiles and the calculation of VIP status, ensuring consistent customer segmentation across different business processes.
