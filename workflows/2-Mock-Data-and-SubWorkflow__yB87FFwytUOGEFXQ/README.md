# 2 Mock Data and SubWorkflow

> This workflow retrieves customer data from a Google Sheet based on an email address and determines their VIP status.

---

## 📌 Overview

This n8n workflow is designed to be executed as a sub-workflow, receiving an input `userEmail`. It queries a specified Google Sheet to retrieve comprehensive customer information, including their rating and total orders.

Upon retrieving the customer's details, the workflow evaluates specific criteria (customer rating and total orders) to determine if the customer qualifies for VIP status. This calculated VIP status is then added to the customer's data.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

The primary purpose of this workflow is to provide a standardized and reusable component for fetching customer data and calculating their VIP status. This enables parent workflows to easily integrate customer segmentation logic without duplicating the data retrieval and status calculation steps.
