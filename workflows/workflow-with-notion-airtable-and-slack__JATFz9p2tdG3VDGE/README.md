# workflow with notion airtable and slack

> Automates lead capture from a webhook into Google Sheets, Slack, Notion, and Airtable.

---

## 📌 Overview

This workflow automates the ingestion and distribution of lead data. It starts by receiving lead information via a webhook and immediately logging the entry into a Google Sheet for primary record-keeping.

If the lead provides company information, the workflow triggers a notification to a Slack channel to alert the team. Subsequently, the lead details are synchronized into Notion and Airtable to ensure the CRM and task tracking systems are up to date.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

To centralize lead management by automatically distributing incoming lead data across communication tools and databases, reducing manual data entry and improving response times.
